LiTong Group Awarded 'BYD Semiconductor Distributor of the Year 2025'

Recognition for outstanding sales performance and technical support excellence across global markets

LiTong Group is proud to announce that we have been selected as BYD Semiconductor's 'Distributor of the Year 2025', recognizing our exceptional performance in sales growth, technical support excellence, and market development across our global operations.

The prestigious award was presented at BYD Semiconductor's annual partner conference in Shenzhen, China, acknowledging LiTong Group's outstanding achievements in the 2024 fiscal year. The recognition highlights our commitment to delivering comprehensive solutions and superior customer service in the power electronics and semiconductor industry.

Record-Breaking Performance

Throughout 2024, LiTong Group achieved remarkable milestones that contributed to this recognition:

  • Sales Growth: 45% year-over-year increase in BYD Semiconductor product sales
  • Market Expansion: Successfully entered 5 new regional markets
  • Customer Base: Expanded customer portfolio by 60%, adding 150+ new clients
  • Technical Support: Delivered over 200 technical training sessions and workshops

The BYD Semiconductor 'Distributor of the Year' award is presented annually to the global distributor that demonstrates excellence in sales performance, technical support, market development, and customer satisfaction.
