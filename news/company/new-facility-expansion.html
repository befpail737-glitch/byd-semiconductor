LiTong Group Expands Operations with New Distribution Center

Enhanced inventory capacity to better serve our growing customer base with faster delivery times

LiTong Group is proud to announce the opening of our new state-of-the-art distribution center in Shenzhen, China. This 50,000 square meter facility represents a significant investment in our logistics infrastructure and underscores our commitment to providing exceptional service to our global customer base.

Strategic Investment

The new Shenzhen distribution center is part of our broader $50 million investment in supply chain infrastructure over the next three years. This expansion enables us to:

  • Reduce Lead Times: Cut average delivery time from 5-7 days to 2-3 days for standard orders
  • Increase Inventory Depth: Maintain larger stock levels of high-volume BYD Semiconductor products
  • Enhance Customer Service: Provide real-time inventory visibility and order tracking
  • Support Growth: Accommodate our expanding customer base and increasing order volumes

Advanced Technology Integration

Automated Systems

The facility incorporates cutting-edge warehouse automation technology:

🤖 Robotic Picking Systems

Real-time inventory tracking with RFID technology and integrated ERP systems for accurate stock levels.

📊 Predictive Analytics

AI-powered demand forecasting to optimize inventory levels and reduce stockouts.

🔍 Quality Assurance

Automated quality checks and environmental monitoring to ensure product integrity.
