Electric Vehicle Manufacturer Reduces Inverter Size by 30%

Automotive IGBT Modules

Challenge: A leading electric vehicle manufacturer needed to reduce the size of their main inverter while maintaining power density and efficiency for their new vehicle platform.

Solution: Our FAE team worked closely with the customer's engineering team to implement BYD's BSM1200D12P2C01s IGBT modules, which offered superior switching performance and thermal characteristics. We provided detailed thermal analysis, layout recommendations, and design review support.

Results: The customer successfully reduced inverter size by 30% while improving efficiency by 1.2%. The solution also reduced manufacturing costs by 8% due to simplified cooling requirements.

Solar Inverter Company Improves Efficiency in 1500V Systems

Renewable Energy SiC MOSFETs

Challenge: A solar inverter manufacturer was transitioning to 1500V systems but struggling with efficiency targets and thermal management in their new designs.

Solution: We recommended BYD's BSC750D65M1G001 SiC MOSFETs for their superior performance at high voltages. Our team provided SPICE models, application notes, and hands-on design support to optimize the switching topology.

Results: The customer achieved 2.1% higher efficiency than their previous solution and reduced the number of cooling components by 40%, resulting in significant BOM cost savings.
