BSC750D65M1G001

650V/75A SiC MOSFET for High-Efficiency Power Conversion

Product Overview

The BSC750D65M1G001 is a high-performance Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFET designed for high-efficiency, high-frequency power conversion applications. This device offers superior switching performance, low on-resistance, and excellent thermal characteristics, making it ideal for automotive, industrial, and renewable energy applications.

Key Features

  • 650V blocking voltage capability
  • 75A continuous drain current
  • Ultra-low on-resistance (RDS(on))
  • High switching frequency capability up to 100kHz
  • Low gate charge for reduced switching losses
  • Excellent thermal performance
  • AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
