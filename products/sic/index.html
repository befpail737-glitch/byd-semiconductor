Frequently Asked Questions - BYD SiC Distributor

Why choose BYD SiC MOSFETs over silicon IGBTs?

BYD SiC MOSFETs offer 3x higher switching frequency, 50% lower switching losses, and superior thermal performance compared to silicon IGBTs. This enables smaller, lighter, and more efficient power systems for EVs, solar inverters, and industrial applications.

What voltage ratings are available for BYD SiC MOSFETs?

BYD SiC MOSFETs are available in 650V, 900V, and 1200V ratings. The 650V series is ideal for OBC and power supplies, 900V for industrial drives, and 1200V for EV traction inverters and high-power solar applications.
