BIM300D75P3I001

750V/300A Intelligent Power Module for Motor Drive Applications

Product Overview

The BIM300D75P3I001 is a high-performance Intelligent Power Module (IPM) that integrates IGBT power devices with optimized gate driver circuits and comprehensive protection functions. This module is designed for three-phase motor drive applications, offering superior performance, reliability, and ease of integration.

Key Features

  • 750V blocking voltage capability
  • 300A continuous collector current
  • Integrated gate driver circuits
  • Built-in protection functions
  • Overcurrent protection (OCP)
  • Over-temperature protection (OTP)
  • Under-voltage lockout (UVLO)
  • Short-circuit protection
  • Low electromagnetic interference
  • Compact package design
