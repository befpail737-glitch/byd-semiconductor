Frequently Asked Questions

Despite its compact size, the BSM150D12P2H001 maintains excellent efficiency with VCE(sat) of just 1.45V. The advanced chip design and optimized packaging ensure thermal performance comparable to larger modules while offering superior power density.

The module is designed for standard cooling methods. While compact, the optimized thermal path and low thermal resistance (0.19¬∞C/W) allow effective heat removal with conventional heatsinks. For high-power applications, forced air cooling is recommended.
