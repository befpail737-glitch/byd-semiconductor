What MCU series does BYD offer?

BYD offers the BF32F100 series (Cortex-M0, entry-level), BF32F200 series (Cortex-M3, mainstream), and BF32F400 series (Cortex-M4, high-performance with FPU). All series are automotive-grade qualified (AEC-Q100) and industrial temperature rated.

Yes, BYD MCUs are AEC-Q100 qualified and designed specifically for automotive applications including body control modules, motor control, battery management systems (BMS), and LED lighting control. They meet automotive reliability and quality standards.

BYD MCUs are supported by standard ARM development tools including Keil MDK, IAR Embedded Workbench, and GCC-based IDEs. We provide free SDK, HAL libraries, and code examples. Our FAE team can assist with code review and debugging.
